How to Submit Application Form

Attorneys

bkl always welcomes career inquiries from attorneys qualified outside Korea who are seeking to practice law in a challenging environment with other attorneys and professionals who are leaders in their respective practice areas. Whether you have just recently graduated law school or are an experienced attorney seeking to expand your practice, if you share core bkl values such as client focus, teamwork, excellence, and integrity, we welcome your application. You may apply by sending us your resume with an English-language cover letter(transcripts and writing samples are optional) using the application form at the bottom of this page. Our recruiting committee will respond to applicants via e-mail within a few weeks.

Interns

bkl offers internships to law school students and university students during the summer and winter months, typically for a period of 1 to 2 months. The common goal of our internship programs is to provide each intern with the opportunity to receive excellent training and to perform meaningful work appropriate to his or her background and experience. Applications for 2017 summer internships are accepted from September 1, 2016 to January 31, 2017, while applications for 2017-2018 winter internships are accepted between July 1, 2017 and September 30, 2017. To apply, please submit your resume with an English-language cover letter (transcripts and writing samples are optional) within the designated application period using the application form at the bottom of this page. Our internship committee will respond to applicants via e-mail within a few weeks.
